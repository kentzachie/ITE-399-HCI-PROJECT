PARENTS PORTAL

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College was established back in 1948 as Parent-Teacher College – a nonpolitical and nonsectarian educational institution. The school served the youth and working individuals by providing education from the primary to the tertiary levels. In the next 30 years, PHINMA COC expanded its course offerings to include Computer Science, Engineering, and Mass Communications.

PHINMA COC was acquired in 2005 by PHINMA, making it one of the conglomerate’s first investments in the educational sector. Through PHINMA’s leadership and management, the college improved its existing academic programs by employing non-traditional approaches to learning.

Improving Study Habits

Here are the 10 ways to improve your study habits:

  • Set Clear Goals: Set specific, achievable goals for each study session.
  • Prioritize Tasks: Tackle the most challenging subjects first.
  • Active Learning: Engage with the material through summarization and teaching.
  • Use Effective Study Techniques: Try flashcards, mind mapping, and quizzes.
  • Take Regular Breaks: Break your study sessions into manageable chunks.
  • Minimize Distractions: Stay focused by eliminating distractions.
  • Seek Help When Needed: Ask for clarification from peers or teachers.
  • Stay Active: Engage in physical activity to boost focus.
  • Eliminate Multitasking: Focus on one task at a time.
  • Reward Yourself: Celebrate achieving study goals.

Everyone’s learning style is different, so explore with these tips to find what works best for you. Consistency is key to developing effective study habits that support your success in academics and career.
