ACCESSIBLE & AFFORDABLE

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College was established back in 1948 as Parent-Teacher College – a nonpolitical and nonsectarian educational institution. The school served the youth and working individuals by providing education from the primary to the tertiary levels. In the next 30 years, PHINMA COC expanded its course offerings to include Computer Science, Engineering, and Mass Communications.

PHINMA COC was acquired in 2005 by PHINMA, making it one of the conglomerate’s first investments in the educational sector. Through PHINMA’s leadership and management, the college improved its existing academic programs by employing non-traditional approaches to learning.

SCHOLARSHIPS

Most importantly, our education is affordable. We do not charge any hidden or unnecessary fees. We support deserving students throughout their tertiary learning years by offering scholarship programs. Our Handog Kaibigan Scholarship Program provides 50% discount on tuition & miscellaneous fees for our more financially-challenged students.

Check our complete list of scholarships below:

Hawak Kamay Scholarship

Benefits:

  • HK5.5 (P5,500 per semester)
  • HK7.5 (as low as P7,500 per semester)
  • HK10 (as low as P10,000 per semester)
  • HK12.5 (as low as P12,500 per semester)
  • *Books and uniforms not included

Eligibility:

  • Incoming freshman
  • Belong to a low-income family
  • Must pass the scholarship exam

Requirements:

  • Form 137
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • NSO Birth Certificate

Maintenance: The scholarship extends for the normal life of the chosen program upon enrollment in first year. Must be able to render 80 hours/semester.
