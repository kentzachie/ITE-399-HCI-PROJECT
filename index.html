CHOOSE PHINMA EDUCATION

As an innovative Philippine business institution, PHINMA believes that access to quality education is the solution to many of our country’s problems.While the enrollment rate in the Philippines has always been high, PHINMA discovered that most students do not finish tertiary education. Students were willing to learn but did not have the resources to stay in school.

That is why in 2004, PHINMA Education was established. PHINMA Education is making education accessible in key growth areas all over the country. It has transformed existing educational institutions to provide better academic, operational, and community support for all of its students. Through its efforts, more students are able to earn college degrees and become globally competitive professionals. The PHINMA Education Network is now composed of nine secondary and tertiary educational institutions spread throughout the country.

ABOUT US

PHINMA Cagayan de Oro College was established back in 1948 as Parent-Teacher College – a nonpolitical and nonsectarian educational institution. The school served the youth and working individuals by providing education from the primary to the tertiary levels. In the next 30 years, PHINMA COC expanded its course offerings to include Computer Science, Engineering, and Mass Communications.

PHINMA COC was acquired in 2005 by PHINMA, making it one of the conglomerate’s first investments in the educational sector. Through PHINMA’s leadership and management, the college improved its existing academic programs by employing non-traditional approaches to learning.

OUR COURSES

College of Education

Train to become an exceptional educator with in-depth coursework in curriculum development, child psychology, and innovative teaching strategies. Hands-on internships ensure practical experience in real classroom settings.

College of Information and Technology Education

Master the latest in software development, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. Gain practical skills through coding projects, industry collaborations, and real-world tech solutions.

College of Engineering and Architecture

Delve into structural engineering, urban planning, and architectural design. Engage in cutting-edge projects that combine creativity and technical expertise.

College of Managing Accountancy

Develop strong financial, auditing, and business management skills. Learn about corporate finance, taxation, and economic strategies for a competitive career in finance.

School of Criminology and Criminal Justice

Explore forensic science, criminal psychology, and law enforcement techniques. Prepare for careers in security, investigation, and justice administration.

College of Allied Health and Sciences

Learn essential medical sciences, patient care, and healthcare management. Gain hands-on experience in clinical settings to prepare for a career in healthcare.
