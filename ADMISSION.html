Ready to take the leap? Follow these enrollment steps to start your journey with PHINMA Education.

Requirements:
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Form 138 (Report Card)

Onsite Enrollment:

  • Fill out your enrollment form at the Marketing Office.
  • Pay the enrollment fee:
    • ₱750 (HK Grantee)
    • ₱1,000 (Regular student)
  • Get your ORF (Official Registration Form) or Class schedule at the assessment area.
  • Go back to the Marketing Office for orientation and get your welcome kit.

Online Enrollment:

  • Fill out this online enrollment form: Register Here
  • NOTE: Please only SELECT ONLINE PAYMENT as your mode of payment.
  • Check your email if you received a notification of your successful registration receipt.
  • Wait for our team to call you and guide you on to the next process.
  • Check email for your class schedule or ORF.
